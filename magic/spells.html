Flesh Knitter

This spell allows the caster to knit flesh together.

Level: Level 5: Transmutation
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 ft
Components: Verbal
Duration: Instant
Classes: Cler, Sorc, War, Wiz

Reknit: If the target has a severed body part within 5 ft of the stub, the body part begins to animate and move back towards its point of origin. Once there, the part will then begin to knit itself back and reattach where it belongs. For each severed body part reattached, the creature receives back 1d8 HP.

Anamnesis

This spell allows the caster to thrust a painful past experience (or memory) onto the target.

Level: Level 4: Enchantment
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V,S
Duration: Instant
Classes: Bard, Sorc, War, Wiz

Tabu La Rasa: The target muct make a Intelligence saving throw. On a fail, the target takes 1d6 phychic damage for each harsh memory transfered. On a save, the target takes half the damage.
